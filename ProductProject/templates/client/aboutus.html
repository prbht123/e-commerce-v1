Founded in 1995, Product has flourished since then. With almost two centuries of experience and traditional knowledge passed on for five generations, we have put in place unique milling and manufacturing processes that ensures best quality and nutrient rich rice grains. From procurement to packaging our time tested methodologies and quality control measures have not only earned us a niche in the market but also a pleasant spot in the hearts of our customers.

The perfect combination of a dedicated skilled workforce with advanced milling and manufacturing technology continues to make our journey successful and remarkable. From grandma’s special khichdi to the chef’s signature biriyani, from the all-perfect mom’s delicious kheer to the never-perfect bachelor’s one pot pulao, Product rice is a household name for every occasion.

We sincerely owe our gratitude to every customer for trusting us and owe it equally to our partners and other stakeholders’ alike. Above all, we see ourselves as a committed member of the society contributing to a larger cause of public wellbeing and social welfare and fulfil our corporate social responsibilities diligently as part of our mission.
